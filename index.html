Use Case Example

How a Vision Pro app creates real business value.

A concrete example helps clients understand that spatial computing is not just visual polish — it can reduce training risk, improve repetition, and make complex decisions easier to practice.

EmergencyRoom Pro

Immersive ER training without the risk of real-world mistakes.

EmergencyRoom Pro shows how an Apple Vision Pro simulator can train teams through realistic patient scenarios, vital signs, decision points, medical tools, and post-session performance scoring.

ProblemMedical training can be expensive, limited, stressful, and difficult to repeat safely with real patients.
Vision Pro SolutionA spatial emergency room with patient status, floating monitors, tools, scenarios, and guided decisions.
AI LayerAdaptive scoring, decision feedback, scenario variation, and automated post-training review.
Business ResultSafer practice, repeatable simulations, clearer evaluation, and a stronger case for institutional adoption.
