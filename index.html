Do we need a full app idea?

No. A rough concept is enough. We can help shape the target user, core scenario, interface direction, and MVP scope.

Can this be used for investors or incubators?

Yes. The brief can be structured as a pitch-ready prototype story with visuals, use cases, and a development roadmap.

The site focuses on strategy, UI, prototype direction, and MVP planning. Development scope can be defined after the brief.
